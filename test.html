Introduction

This ePub 3 with a SMIL audio overlay is a demonstration of an audio book designed for both accessibility and entertainment.

Let's start off by giving our thanks and appreciation to the LibriVox project in general, and the generous book readers in particular, for making so many books audio accessible to all.

If you are not particularly interested in the technical issues of AudioOverlays using SMIL, just move on to the next page and enjoy the bookfor its wonderful writing and brilliant story telling, richly enhanced by the dramatic reading by theLibriVox team.

Text highlighting

This version of AZARDI Online highlights text in syncronisation with the audio. At present there is no option to turn highlighting off.

The SMIL tagging has been applied at four resolutions:

Paragraph level interaction and highlighting. With the exception of Stave/Chapter One, All Stave/Chapters have been SMIL tagged with millisecond accuracy at the paragraph level. A paragraph can be clicked and read, and in continuous reading, highlighting is by the paragraph. Because of the uneven length of paragraphs, this gives a somewhat dislocated view of the narrative.

and...

Sentence level interaction and highlighting. Stave/Chapter 1 is tagged with millisecond accuracy at what we call the major terminator level. That is full-stops, exclamation marks and question marks. This gives a more even highlighted reading experience.

and...

Phrase level interaction and highlighting. Stave/Chapter 5 is tagged with millisecond accuracy at what we call the minor terminator and joiner level. This means the interaction and highlighting is even more granular on major terminators plus: commas, colons, semi-colons, ellipses, en-dashes and em-dashes. This highlighting works well with expressive reading, but can be irritating for click/tap interaction or classroom reading, where probably the paragraph is more useful.

and...

Word/Space level interaction and highlighting. This introduction has been tagged with millisecond accuracy at the word level. That means every word is highlighted individually and gives an irritating flashing effect. This is best reserved for simple children's readers.

EPub 3 means there is likely to be more audio books released. Like all presentation issues in publishing, there are decisions to be made. This small sample demonstrates SMIL tagging granularity options, and the appropriateness and usefulness of each approach for any particular content.
